Methodology

This report uses the OFAC SDN (Specially Designated Nationals) list, known mixer/tumbler databases, and on-chain transaction analysis to generate a composite risk score. The scoring engine evaluates five dimensions: direct sanctions matches, first-degree sanctions exposure, mixer interactions, transaction velocity patterns, and new wallet inflow behavior.
